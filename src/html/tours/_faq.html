Frequently Asked Questions:

  • What should I pack for my tour?

    Comfortable clothing, insect repellent, sunscreen, and a hat are essential. We provide a detailed packing list for each tour.

  • How do I book a tour?

    You can book directly through our website or contact our customer service team for assistance.

  • What kind of accommodations are provided?

    Our tours offer eco-friendly lodges and comfortable camping options, all designed to immerse you in the natural environment.

  • Are the tours suitable for children?

    Yes, we offer family-friendly tours with activities suitable for all ages. Please check the specific tour details for age recommendations.

  • What measures are in place for safety?

    We adhere to strict safety protocols, provide professional guides, and ensure all equipment is regularly checked and maintained.

  • Can I customize my tour?

    Absolutely! We offer a range of additional services and activities to tailor your experience to your preferences.
