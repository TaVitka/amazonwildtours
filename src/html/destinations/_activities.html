Activities and Experiences

Wildlife Viewing

  • Spot jaguars, tapirs, monkeys, and countless bird species.
  • Best Locations: Manu National Park, Yasuni National Park

Birdwatching

  • The Amazon is a birdwatcher's paradise with hundreds of species to observe.
  • Best Locations: Amazon River Basin, Manu National Park

Jungle Treks

  • Guided treks through the dense jungle to discover hidden waterfalls and diverse ecosystems.
  • Best Locations: Amazon River Basin, Manu National Park

Cultural Encounters

  • Engage with indigenous communities and learn about their way of life.
  • Best Locations: Yasuni National Park, Amazon River Basin

River Cruises

  • Cruise the Amazon and its tributaries, enjoying the scenic beauty and abundant wildlife.
  • Best Locations: Amazon River Basin, Yasuni National Park
