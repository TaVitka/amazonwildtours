Geography, Climate, and Biodiversity:

  • Located in southeastern Peru, Manu National Park spans from the Andean highlands to the Amazon lowlands, showcasing diverse climates and ecosystems.

Highlighted Attractions:

  • Cloud Forest: Explore the mystical cloud forests teeming with life.
  • Clay Licks: Watch macaws and parrots gather at clay licks.

Popular Activities:

  • Trekking: Hike through varied terrains from mountains to rainforests.
  • Wildlife Viewing: Spot monkeys, jaguars, and hundreds of bird species.

Traveler Testimonials:

  • "Manu National Park is a true gem. The wildlife sightings and the untouched nature make it a must-visit." - Sarah L.
