Geography, Climate, and Biodiversity:

  • The Amazon River Basin covers over 7 million square kilometers, with a tropical climate that supports an extraordinary range of species.

Highlighted Attractions:

  • River Cruises: Explore the river's vast network of waterways.
  • Wildlife Reserves: Visit protected areas to see diverse wildlife.

Popular Activities:

  • Birdwatching: Spot exotic birds in their natural habitat.
  • Fishing: Try your hand at catching piranhas and other native fish.
