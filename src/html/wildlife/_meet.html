Meet the Icons of the Amazon

  • Jaguar

    The largest predator in the Amazon, known for its strength and stealth. Found in dense rainforests and along riverbanks.

  • Macaw

    These vibrant birds are a symbol of the Amazon, known for their bright plumage and loud calls.

  • Anaconda

    One of the world's largest snakes, often found in the Amazon's swamps and slow-moving rivers.
